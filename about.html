About CODEX

CODEX is a student-driven platform designed to centralize college study materials for engineering students. Born out of the frustration of scattered notes and late-night "bro notes bhej na" messages, this hub organizes notes, previous year papers, lab manuals, and reference books by branch, semester, and subject. Our goal is to make exam preparation less chaotic and more efficient, ensuring you have everything you need in one accessible place.

Whether you're cramming for midterms or prepping for finals, CODEX provides curated, exam-focused resources contributed by students like you. We prioritize quality, organization, and ease of use, with features like real-time search, filtering by branch/semester, and direct download links to Google Drive folders.

Frequently Asked Questions

📚 What is CODEX?
CODEX is a free, student-driven platform that centralizes college study materials including notes, previous year question papers, lab manuals, and reference books organized by branch, semester, and subject.
💰 Is CODEX really free?
Yes! CODEX is completely free with no subscriptions, hidden fees, or advertisements. We believe education should be accessible to everyone.
👤 Do I need to create an account?
You can browse as a guest, but creating a free account helps you save favorites, track your progress, and access exclusive features.
📥 Can I upload my own materials?
Yes! We encourage students to contribute. You can upload notes, PYQs, lab manuals, and other study materials to help your peers. All uploads are reviewed for quality.
🔒 Is my data safe?
We take privacy seriously. Your personal information is encrypted and never shared with third parties. We don't track or sell your data.
📱 Can I access CODEX on mobile?
Yes! CODEX is fully responsive and works on all devices - smartphones, tablets, and desktops. You can access your materials anytime, anywhere.
🤝 How can I contribute?
Sign up, go to your dashboard, and use the "Upload Materials" feature. Add title, subject, semester, and upload your file. Our team reviews it within 24 hours.
❌ What if I find broken or outdated material?
Click the "Report Issue" button on any material. Our team will verify and fix it quickly. Helping us improve makes CODEX better for everyone!
